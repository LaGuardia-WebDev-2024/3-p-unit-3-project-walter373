Lomo Saltado

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Sirloin Steak 1 lb
Tomatoes 2
Red Onions 2
French Fries Pre cooked
Garlic Cloves 2

Step 1: Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan over high heat. Add the steak, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned, 5-6 minutes. Remove from the pan.
Step 2: Heat the remaining tablespoon of oil in the same pan, then add the red onion and cook for about 5 minutes, until softened and browned. Add the tomato, garlic, and aji amarillo paste, and cook for another 5-7 minutes, until the tomatoes have released some of their juices, but are still intact.
Step 3: Add the soy sauce and vinegar and stir to combine, let cook for 1 minute.
Step 4: Add the steak, fries, and cilantro. Toss gently to coat the fries in the sauce.
Step 5: Serve with rice, if desired.

Chicken Quesadilla

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
boneless skinless chicken breast 1/2 lb
bell pepper, green, red, or yellow peppers, julienned 1/2 cup
white onion, julienned 1/2 cup
large flour tortillas 2
shredded cheddar cheese, 2 cups

Step 1:In a large skillet, coat chicken with salt, pepper, chili powder, cumin, cayenne, and garlic powder. Cook 5-7 minutes.
Step 2:Add the peppers and onions and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until they are soft. Remove chicken, onions and peppers from the pan and set aside.
Step 3:Place the tortilla in the skillet and add a layer of cheese on half of the tortilla.
Step 4: Add cooked chicken, peppers and onions. Top with more cheese and fold the tortilla in half.
Step 5: Cook for 6 minutes over medium heat, flipping half way.
Step 6:Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with pico de gallo, guacamole, and sour cream on the side if you please.

Source: Tasty.com
